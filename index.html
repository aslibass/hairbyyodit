Common questions

What do I need to have ready at home?
A power point near a chair or stool, somewhere with daylight if possible, and a stretch of floor I can put down a drop cloth on. I bring everything else.
How long does an appointment take?
Cut and finish is about ninety minutes. Foils run two and a half to three hours depending on length. I'll tell you on booking what to expect for your specific job.
Can I have pets / kids / music?
Yes to all. Pets stay where they're comfortable, kids are fine — I've done cuts with three under five in the room. Bring your music.
What if I'm not sure what I want?
That's the restyle appointment — longer consultation, time to work it out together. I'd rather start there than guess.
Do you do weddings outside Adelaide?
Yes, by negotiation. Travel beyond Greater Adelaide is quoted with your enquiry and there's usually a minimum party size.
How do I pay?
Bank transfer, card via Square reader, or cash on the day. Wedding bookings take a 25% deposit at the trial.
